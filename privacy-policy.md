**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the gas-ph app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Renjo Angeles (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

**Information Collection and Use**

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

*   The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
*   The time spent on the Application
*   The operating system you use on your mobile device

**Location Data**

The Application collects precise location data (GPS coordinates, including latitude and longitude) from your mobile device. This data is collected when you:

*   Submit fuel price reports
*   Submit station reports
*   Submit EV station reports

Your precise coordinates are stored alongside these reports to associate them with the correct gas station or EV station location. In addition, the Application uses your location to provide nearby station results and other location-based features.

**Data Stored in Supabase**

The following data is collected and stored in the Service Provider's Supabase backend:

*   **Account profile:** email address, full name, username, and avatar
*   **Price reports and confirmations:** fuel price submissions and user confirmations, tied to your user ID
*   **Station reports:** reports about gas station status or conditions, including GPS coordinates
*   **EV station reports:** reports about electric vehicle charging stations, including GPS coordinates
*   **Favorites and notification preferences:** your saved stations and notification settings
*   **Push notification tokens:** your Expo push notification token, including device token and platform identifier
*   **Station photos:** photos you upload of stations, with user attribution
*   **Feedback messages:** feedback you submit through the Application, along with your device ID
*   **Username change history:** a log of changes to your username

**On-Device Storage**

The Application uses MMKV to store data locally on your device, including authentication tokens, cached location data, and user preferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless required for Application functionality.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that the Service Provider requests will be retained by them and used as described in this privacy policy.

**Third-Party Access**

The Application utilizes third-party services that receive specific data as described below:

*   **Sentry** — receives your user ID and error context (such as stack traces and app state at the time of a crash) for error monitoring and diagnostics. Sentry does **not** receive your IP address or other personally identifiable information.
*   **RevenueCat** — receives your user ID, subscription status, and purchase history to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases.
*   **AdMob** — receives standard Google ad targeting data (such as device identifiers and ad interaction data) to serve advertisements within the Application.
*   **Supabase** — serves as the backend data storage for the Application. All user data listed in the "Data Stored in Supabase" section above is stored using this service.
*   **Expo** — receives your push notification token to deliver push notifications to your device.
*   **Google Maps Platform** — receives location queries to display maps and station locations within the Application.
